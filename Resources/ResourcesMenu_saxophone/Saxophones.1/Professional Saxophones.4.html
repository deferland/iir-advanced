Professional Saxophones

Professional level saxophones represent the finest in quality and craftsmanship. These instruments are generally handmade, and have higher quality metal, pads, springs, posts, and other components. They also maintain their regulation better, leading to fewer leaks. Overall, these saxophones have richer, more beautiful sounds, and better intonation and evenness of tone throughout the range. Below is a list of recommended professional saxophones. My personal favorites include the Selmer Series III or Series II, or Yamaha Custom 875. The Selmer Reference and Yamaha Custom Z saxophones are also very fine new instruments created specifically with an ear towards jazz and popular music.

  • Selmer Series III
  • Selmer Series II
  • Selmer Reference 54 or 36
  • Yamaha Custom 875 EX
  • Yamaha Custom 875 Z
  • Yamaha YAS/YTS/YBS-62
  • Keilwerth SX-90R
  • Yanagisawa A991/992
  • Buffet Prestige S3
  • Cannonball Big Bell Stone Series/Vintage Pro
