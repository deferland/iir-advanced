Classical reeds

Following is a list of commonly-used reeds for classical work, all featuring a French cut. The Vandoren Traditional is arguably the most commonly-used reed for classical music, and is my personal preference. On alto I use a #3 or #3.5, and similar strengths on the other saxophones.

  • Vandoren Traditional
  • Vandoren V12
  • Rico Reserve
  • Alexander Classique
  • Rico Royal
  • Hemke
  • Legere (synthetic)
