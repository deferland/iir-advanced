Jazz Reeds

There is a quite a bit of variety in commonly-used jazz reeds. Professionals may use one brand of reed on one instrument or mouthpiece, and a different brand on a different setup. As a general rule, you want to find a reed that is relatively free-blowing and provides a full sound for jazz ensemble work, while not being too edgy. The following list is a good starting point for reeds to consider on a jazz mouthpiece. As you work on improving your tone, experiment with different brands and strengths of reeds until you find one that works well with your setup.

  • Vandoren (Java, ZZ, Java Red, V16)
  • Rico Jazz Select
  • Lavoz
  • Alexander (DC, Superial, NY)
  • Francois Louis
  • Hemke
  • Rico
