Open hole/closed hole (plateau/French)

All students who start out should start on a closed holed instrument. If the student is young and has very small fingers, it might be difficult to cover the holes on the keys. As the student progresses and is ready for the next step up in the level of flutes, I would highly recommend an open holed flute. For students who start out on closed hole flutes, I would not recommend getting one that was sterling silver. The money would be more efficiently spent on an open holed flute with sterling silver headjoint or body.
