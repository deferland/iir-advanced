Sterling silver vs silver plated

Generally, the more silver the flute has, the better the sound quality and value of the flute. Usually when a flute is silver plated, the main material is nickel with a silver coating on it. Beginner flutes and those just starting out do not need to worry as much about the amount of silver in their flutes, but as the student progresses beyond the first flute, you will want to consider buying a silver flute. If you can, try for at least a sterling silver headjoint and lip plate and silver plated body and keys.
