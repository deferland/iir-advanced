Inline/offset G key

This refers to the key that is used by the left hand ring finger. All student flutes have an offset G key to compensate for the smaller hands of young flutists. Traditionally, as the students grow, the need for an offset G key has diminishes and the more advanced flutes are made with an inline G key. However, the offset G key is now requested more often in professional flutes because it adds a bit of relief to the hand. I would recommend looking for a flute with an offset G key unless the student has rather large hands.
