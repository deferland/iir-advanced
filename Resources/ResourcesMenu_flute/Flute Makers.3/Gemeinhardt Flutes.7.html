Gemeinhardt Flutes

Gemeinhardt is the standard name in student flutes. They are very popular among music educators across the country. They are durable, solid instruments and require minimal maintenance.

Gemeinhardt 2SP

Student model, all silver plated, Plateau or closed hole, offset G, C footjoint.

Gemeinhardt 2SH

Student model, sterling silver headjoint, silver plated body, footjoint and mechanism, Plateau or closed hole, offset G, C footjoint.

Gemeinhardt 3SB

Sterling silver headjoint, body and footjoint, silver plated mechanism, French or open hole, inline G, B footjoint.

Gemeinhardt 33SB/SSBO (inline or offset G)

Sterling silver headjoint, body and footjoint, silver plated mechanism, French or open hole, inline/offset G, B footjoint, pointed key arms, special "J" model thin-wall headjoint, gold springs.

Gemeinhardt KGM Standard

Sterling silver H1*R thinwall heajdoint with rose gold riser and gold-plated lip plate, sterling silver body and footjoint, silver plated keys, French or open hole, inline G, pointed key arms, drawn tone holes, 12K white gold springs, B footjoint.
