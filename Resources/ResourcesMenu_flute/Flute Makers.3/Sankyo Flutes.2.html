Sankyo Flutes

Sankyo's are known for their signature sound and evenness of technique. The shape of the lip plate allows for better air focus, and is ideal for the intermediate student who is developing a refined and polished sound. These handmade flutes are a great value and very popular. I have played Sankyo flutes and have very satisfied students with them.

Sankyo CF-201BN/BON

Etude model: Sterling silver handcut headjoint, silver plated nickel silver body, footjoint and mechanism, French or open hole, inline/offset G, B footjoint, pointed key arms, NEL, drawn tone holes, silver plated finish, 0.40mm tubing

Sankyo CF-301BN/BON
