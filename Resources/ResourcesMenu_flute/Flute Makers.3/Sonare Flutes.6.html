Sonare Flutes

This is Powell Flutes' intermediate line of instruments. Both the body and headjoint of the Sonare are made at Powell Flutes, and the result is an instrument with the signature Powell sound, with a fluid and solid mechanism, for a reasonable price. For added resonance and projection Sonare uses gold seal (TM) trill resonator pads.
