Avanti Flutes

The Avanti flute company is a product of a collaboration of the professional flute company Brannen and Conn-Selmer, and the result is a synthesis of the smooth mechanism and robust sound reminiscent of Brannen flutes, and the reliability and cost-effectiveness of the Conn-Selmer company of student instruments.

Avanti flutes are great for the intermediate flutist who is ready for an open-holed instrument and wants to work on developing a rich, full tone. The mechanism is very smooth and the instruments have the look and feel of a professional flute but at a reasonable price. The sound on the Avanti flute is rich and solid, good for the intermediate and advanced intermediate flutist who has good breath control and support.
