Haynes Flutes

Haynes flutes are one of the 3 major Boston-based flute manufacturers (along with Powell and Brannen) that dominate the professional market. Of the 3 companies, the Haynes flute has a rich history of world-renowned advocates, including Jean-Pierre Rampal.

Haynes flutes are known and revered for their rich, colorful sound. I have assisted many students in the purchase of new instruments and time and time again they are drawn to and choose the beauty of the Haynes sound. These instruments have the capacity to draw out the very best of the musicians and many of my students have made immediate and significant improvements in their playing after the purchase of a Haynes flute.

Haynes flutes are my personal favorite flutes as a professional, and they have recently come out with a line of intermediate flutes, called Haynes Classic. These flutes are great for the aspiring professional high school student who wants to transform their playing and take it to the next level.
