Azumi Flutes

The Azumi flute is made by Altus, a Japanese flute company which produces professional flutes. Among the Japanese flutes for intermediate flutists of middle and high school age, the Azumi company produces the highest quality. The headjoint is based on the Altus "Z" cut which focuses the sound beautifully, and a great component to the intermediate level flute. The flute is very easy to play and best for the advanced beginner and intermediate student who is still learning and developing breath support and control. It is difficult to produce a big complex sound, so it may not be the best for more developed players.

Azumi AZ2000RBS/RBSO (inline/offset G)

Britannia Silver (.958) handcut "Z" cut Altus headjoint, silver plated nickel silver body and keys, french or open hole, inline/offset G, B footjoint. Optional split E mechanism, which allows for better intonation on high E.

Azumi AZ3000RBS/RBSO (inline/offset G)

Same as 2000 model but with silver body.
