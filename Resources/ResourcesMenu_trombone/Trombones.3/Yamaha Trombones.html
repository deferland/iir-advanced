Yamaha Trombones

Yamaha is an instrument maker based out of Japan. Many successful artists perform on their instruments, including some members of the Vienna Philharmonic, Boston Symphony and Seattle Symphony. Yamaha instruments have long been known for their ease of response and vibration. Yamaha is also a brand that is very popular with beginners. They make very good student model instruments. In my opinion, Yamaha is a company that seems to be consistently improving their product.

Yamaha instruments are available through retail stores and are very reasonably priced, especially compared to Edwards and Shires. In my opinion, the new Yamaha Xeno is the best professional tenor trombone that Yamaha has produced, and many high level players are fans of this instrument.

  • Yamaha YSL 8220 Xeno Series- Large bore professional model.
  • Yamaha YSL 620 600 Series- Large bore professional model.
  • Yamaha YSL 354- Small bore student model.
