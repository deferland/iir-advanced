Conn Trombones

Conn is probably the oldest recognizable name in American trombone making. The company was based out of Elkhart, Indiana. In the 70's, Conn trombones were the primary instruments of choice for most orchestral trombonists, and they continue to be popular today all over the world. Christian Lindberg, perhaps the most recognizable name in the trombone industry is a Conn artist. The 88H is the most popular professional instrument made by Conn.
