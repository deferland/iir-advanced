Edwards Trombones

Edwards Instruments is a custom maker based out of Elkhorn, Wisconson. You can order their instruments through their website at www.edwards-instruments.com or by visiting their shop in Elkhorn. I have performed exclusively on Edwards trombones for the last 7 years and can attest to the very high quality of their products. One of the best things about Edwards is the consistency in the quality of their product.

For many years now, Edwards has been the choice of many orchestral trombone players. Edwards trombones are custom made and are geared towards advanced/professional players. Their instruments are played by musicians in many of the world's leading ensembles including the Metropolitan Opera Orchestra, New York Philharmonic and Philadelphia Orchestra. I give Edwards my highest recommendation.

The options available by Edwards is too large to list here. The best way to get an understanding of their available equipment is to visit their website or go to the factory. I would recommend a variation of the T350-E model.
