Bach Trombones

Bach is one of the most popular trombone brands in the world. They make a wide range of instruments for all levels of playing. Bach is the chosen brand of many professional players including artists in the Chicago and San Francisco Symphonies.

Bach instruments are less costly than Edwards and Shires, but also are a little less consistent from instrument to instrument. Unfortunately, not all Bach trombones play the same. Bach instruments are sold in retail stores all around the country. While I do recommend these instruments to students and professionals alike, I would HIGHLY recommend thoroughly trying out and comparing Bach instruments before committing to a particular instrument.

  • Bach 42- Large bore professional model.
  • Bach 36- Medium bore professional model
  • Bach 16 and 12- Small bore professional models.
  • Bach TB200- Student/Intermediate model
