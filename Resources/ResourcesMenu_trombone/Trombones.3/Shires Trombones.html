Shires Trombones

Shires makes a very high quality professional instrument. Steve Shires, the designer of the instrument, worked for Edwards before creating his own company. Although Shires and Edwards are different instruments, there are many similarities in design and playability. Shires have become increasingly popular over the last ten years with orchestral players in the United States. Shires instruments can be purchased at a limited number of retail stores. You can also visit their website at www.seshires.com. If you are interested in purchasing a Shires trombone, I would highly recommend scheduling an appointment to visit their factory outside of Boston. Since Shires trombones are customized, it's best to go to the factory so that you may try the many options that they have available.
