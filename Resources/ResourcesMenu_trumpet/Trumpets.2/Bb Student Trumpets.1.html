Bb Student Trumpets

Starting on a quality instrument is important for trumpet players. Finding success in the first year of playing is essential to long term development and establishment of basic playing technique. It is important that students find an instrument that will allow proper playing. Just as important is a well made instrument that is sturdy and can withstand the day to day riggers of young students.

Below are some of the more popular beginning trumpet choices available. These trumpets are all made with young students in mind and are suitable for their first years of playing. Many of these products are sold with starter kits including cleaning and maintenance items that are needed on a day to day basis.

NOTE: It is NOT recommended that you should ever purchase a cheap trumpet from a supercenter or wholesale warehouse. These instruments are made overseas from the cheapest materials possible and often break within the first year if not the first few months. They are also nearly impossible to tune properly with an ensemble and delay your student's musical development.

Bach TR300

On of the most popular student trumpets on the market today; .459" bore, 4-3/4" two piece bell with rim wire, monel metal pistions, first valve slide thumb hook and adjustable third valve slide ring with stop mechanism, lacquered finish (also in silver plate finish)

Bach TR500

A recent addition to the student Bach line, this instrument is an affordable option for beginning players; .460" bore, nickel silver trim, fixed third slide finger ring and first slide thumb hook, nickel-silver pistions, red brass mouthpipe, clean lacquer finish (also in silver plate finish)

Yamaha YTR-2335

Professional style bell and bore size make this the ideal model for beginning students; .459" bore, 4-7/8" bell pluzuma welded, nickel plated pistons (monel optional), gold brass leadpipe, 1st valve thumb saddle and 3rd valve adjustable slide, gold epoxy lacquer or silver finish

Conn 23B USA Series

From one of the oldest continuous manufacturers of band instruments in America; .462" bore, rose brass leadpipe, monel valves, 4.8" yellow brass bell, 1st valve thumb saddle and 3rd valve adjustable ring with slide stop, comes in either lacquer or silver finish

Jupiter 600L

.460" bore, 4.8" bell, adjustable 3rd valve slide and 1st valve slide thumb saddle, stainless steel pistons, lacquered brass finish

Holton T602

Solid pleasing tone with positive and quick valve action and durable construction; .460" bore, 4.8" diameter plasma welded, monel pistions, adjustable finger ring on 3rd slide and 1st slide thumb saddle, clear baked epoxy lacquer

Blessing B127

Just right for the quality-conscious buyer looking for a bargain; .460" bore, 5" diameter bell, red brass mouthpipe, third valve slide finger ring, first valve thumb hook, monel pistions, lacquer finish
