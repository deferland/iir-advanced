Giddings & Webster

Ivan Giddings has his masters in tuba performance from Pennsylvania State University, and is a tuba teacher and performer by trade. Robert Webster has been working as a machinist and a toolmaker for over thirty years. Bob and Ivan met in November 2000, each with their first prototype stainless steel mouthpieces. They were impressed with what the other could do, and the Giddings & Webster collaboration began.

I have working with Giddings and Webster in collaboration to develop my own mouthpiece. Please read below about their line of trumpet mouthpieces.
