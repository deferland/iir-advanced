Jazz/Lead Mouthpieces

Any mouthpiece can be used for jazz, but certain mouthpieces are preferred by commercial or lead players. These mouthpieces' smaller diameter sizes and shallower cups make it easier to play in the upper register and also to 'cut' through with a brighter tone.

Note: These pieces will improve the upper register but aren't a simple solution to range issues. I would caution a beginning student about using these mouthpieces regularly, as they can possibly make the lower registers less full or give them a very bright or edgy sound

Marcinkiewicz

Shew E5, E14, E91 Ingram E12.4

Schilke
