Curry Mouthpieces

Mark Curry lives in Reno, NV and produces his own line of mouthpieces. The curry mouthpieces correspond to the Bach sizes. However, they also have a "BC" series, which is a Bach "c" rim with a Bach "b" underpart. There are many with deeper cups as well as shallower cups, but the following would be the most standard:

  • 1.25BC
  • 1.25C
  • 3BC
  • 3C
  • 5BC
  • 5C
  • 7BC
  • 7C
