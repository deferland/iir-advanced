Use of a Thumb Saddle

Not every note on the trumpet is perfectly in-tune. Some notes tend to be very sharp, and require the student to adjust the pitch using the valve slides. The thumb saddle is used to adjust pitches for the notes played with the 1st valve (or a combination using it).

Some common notes requiring adjusting are:

  • Bottom line E
  • First space F
  • 2nd space A
  • Top line F
  • High A
