Bore Sizes

Trumpets will come with different sized bores that can affect how the horn sounds and feels. A smaller bore size will be easier to play, but won't have the same breadth of sound that a large bore has. A large bore might have a big sound, but the soft and delicate playing will be more difficult to achieve. I play on a medium large bore trumpet, because I like the sound but it also has lots of flexibility and good softs.
