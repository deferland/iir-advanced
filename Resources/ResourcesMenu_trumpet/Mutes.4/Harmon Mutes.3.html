Harmon mutes

Also known as the "Wah-wah" mute,the harmon mute creates a higher-pitched "buzz" and is popular in jazz and commercial playing. The mute comes in two parts, the mute body and a stem. The stem can be adjusted or removed to change the sound of the mute. Also, the hand can be manipulated over the end of the stem to create a "wah-wah" sound.
