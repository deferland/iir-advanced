Cup mutes

The cup mute is similar to the straight mute, except there is a lip that forms a "cup-like" shape around the bell of the trumpet. This produces a warmer, softer, and more muffled sound then the straight mute.I recommend getting a cup mute that comes with an adjustable cup that allows the trumpeter to control the sound of the mute.
