Practice mutes

In quiet apartments or hotels, the sound of a loud trumpet can be somewhat unwelcome. Thankfully, there are a variety of mutes that can drastically dampen the sound of the trumpet. However, be careful when playing with practice mutes, because they constrict your ability to blow freely. Too much practicing on these mutes can be deconstructive, as the excess backpressure can cause you to develop some bad habits. However, if there is no other option, I highly recommend these mutes.
