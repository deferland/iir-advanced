Student/Intermediate Clarinet

The following clarinets are good for the ease of use and intro to playing. Any of these clarinets are great for the beginner clarinetist looking to play a brand that has a great reputation. They are generally made for their ease of play and production of sound. They may vary in the type of material used to make them and specifications but have the branding that makes them reliable.
