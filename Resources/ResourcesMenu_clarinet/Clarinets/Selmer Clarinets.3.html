Selmer Clarinets

Recital

The Recital is a wonderful clarinet. It is a bit thicker than other clarinets and has a very dark sound. It is great for chamber music as well as orchestral playing.

Signature

These clarinets are known for their improved intonation and nice dark sound. They have a nice ring and project very well.

10-G-A

Little bit of an older model clarinet this clarinet has the characteristics that the Selmers are known for, dark focused sounds.
