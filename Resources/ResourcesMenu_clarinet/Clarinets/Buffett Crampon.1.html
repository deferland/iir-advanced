Buffett Crampon

Tosca

One of Buffet's newest offerings and is recommended for its warmer singing tone and projection. Made in Grenadilla wood or Green line material, this clarinet is a composite of different materials that holds in colder conditions. The Tosca clarinet is available in Bb and A.

Festival

This clarinet belongs to the R13 bore family and has a rich tone. Similar to the R13 but some have a warmer tone. A very flexible clarinet that is easy to blow and fun to play in my opinion.

Vintage

A new take on the 1950's R13's which were famous for their beautiful tone.
The D/A and G#/C# tone holes have been raised

RC Prestige

The body is in M'Pingo wood. These clarinets are recommended for their rich beautiful sound.

RC

Developed in 1975 by the luthier Robert Carree, the RC clarinet has a sweeter sound.

R13 Prestige

A rich sound and is know for being a bit darker and richer than the R13.

R13

Developed in 1955 under the guidance of Robert Carree, the R13 clarinet is a standard choice of clarinetists all around the world. It is recommended for clarinetists looking for tradition and excellence with a focused ringy sound. Also available in green line.
