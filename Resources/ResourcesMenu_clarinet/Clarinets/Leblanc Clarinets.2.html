Leblanc Clarinets

Legacy

One of Leblanc's newest ventures, this clarinet model was designed by Morrie Backun. The Legacy features a grenadilla wood body and comes with Backun traditional barrels and bells in both cocobolo and grenadilla. This is recommended for the clarinetist looking for something fresh. It has a combination of projection and warmth with a unique resonant tone. It is important to try various combinations of barrels and bells if possible as they may change the sound.

Symphonie

This clarinet is similar to the Legacy also designed by Backun. It is a very flexible clarinet that has a more traditional sound character.

Concerto II

Recommended for its colorful sound and ease of play it is a clarinet that bridges old and new.

Opus II

The Opus II is known for its beautiful tone, its hold in the louder dynamics and ease of play.
